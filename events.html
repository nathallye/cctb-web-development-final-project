Elevate Your Events with Our Dessert Tables and Boxes!

At Manaly's Dessert Delivery, we believe that desserts are the perfect way to make any event extra special. Our custom dessert tables and boxes are designed to be the highlight of your celebration, offering a delectable array of sweet treats that will impress your guests and enhance your occasion.

What We Offer

Customized Dessert Tables

Whether you’re hosting a wedding, corporate event, birthday party, or any other gathering, our dessert tables can be tailored to fit the theme and style of your event. Choose from a selection of our gourmet cakes, pastries, and confections to create a spread that’s both beautiful and delicious. We also offer custom packages that include dessert bar setups, designed to match your event theme. These packages feature menu signs, stands, delivery, and cleanup. We require a rectangular table with a cloth on it to be ready for setup. Additionally, we provide donut walls, including our custom donut wall that can hold up to 30 donuts.

Deluxe Dessert Boxes

For a more personalized and portable dessert option, our deluxe dessert boxes are perfect. Each box is filled with a curated selection of our finest treats, including cakes, cookies, pastries, and other confections. These boxes can be customized to suit your event's theme and are a delightful way to thank your guests or provide a sweet takeaway.

Features: Beautifully packaged boxes with a variety of desserts, customizable options to match your event theme, and convenient for guests to take home.
